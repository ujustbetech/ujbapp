Tip

For each product or service that you offer,
please enter a referral amount or referral percentage.
Amount or percentage can be either single or slab-wise.
Please see the example if you need more clarity.

For example, If your product is home loans
Scenario 1 : Referral Amount
Product: Home Loan
Single Referral Amount: Rs. 200
Slab-wise Referral Amount:
Loan of Rs. 0 to 5 Lakhs : Rs. 50,000
Loan of Rs. 5 Lakhs to 10 Lakhs : Rs. 1 Lakh
and so on.

Scenario 2 : Referral Percentage
Product: Home Loan
Single Referral Percentage: 2 %
Slab-wise Referral Percentage
Loan of Rs. 0 to 5 Lakhs : 5%
Loan of Rs. 5 Lakhs to 10 Lakhs : 10%
and so on.
