WHEREAS :-

A. UJustBe has developed and launched a mobile application (“ UJustBe APP/ APP”). B. A person/ entity can apply for listing only through a Partner. A Partner who introduces another member to the APP is tagged as its mentor (“ Mentor”).

C. For each successful referral which is translated into a business for the Listed Partner, the Listed Partners are required to share a fixed percentage of the business acquired being the Referral Fees (defined below).
